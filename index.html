Rules

  • Each player selects how much they will bet this turn
  • Each player is dealt 2 cards
  • They have the option to hit - receive a card - or stand - end your turn
  • In certain cases the player can Split or Double
  • Split - Seperate two equal cards into two different hands
  • Double - Receive one card and double your wager
