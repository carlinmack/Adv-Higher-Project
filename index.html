Rules

  • Each player selects how much they will bet on the next turn
  • Each player is dealt 2 cards
  • Ace is worth 1 or 11, face cards are worth 10 and all other cards are worth the value listed on the card
  • The goal of each round is to get as close to 21 without going bust. All players play against the dealer.
  • Each player has the option to hit and receive a card, or stand and end their turn
  • In certain cases, the player can double their wager and receive a single card
